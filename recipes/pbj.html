Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A delicious sandwich made from soft white bread, salty rich peanut butter, and sweet jam.

Ingredients

  • Bread of any kind
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam or Jelly of any desired flavor

Steps

  1. Grab two pieces of bread.
  2. Using a knife spread Peanut Butter along one side of one slice of bread.
  3. Using a knife spead Jam along one side of the ohter slice of bread
  4. Press the two sides holding our delicioous ingredients to gether
  5. Enjoy a nice snack.
