Grilled Cheese

Description

A delicious sandwich gooey cheese and white bread that complements a nice end to a long day.

Ingredients

  • Bread of any kind
  • Cheese of any kind
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grab two pieces of bread.
  2. Put your two slices of bread together and have a piece or more of cheese between them.
  3. Butter your bread and a pan.
  4. Put the pan over medium heat.
  5. Place your sandwich in the pan.
  6. Wait until desired cripsyness presents itself then flip and repeat for other side.
  7. Remove your sandwich wait for it to cool and have a delicious meal.
